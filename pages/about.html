About us

highly respected embroidery digitizing service

Moon Digitizing is a highly respected embroidery digitizing service provider that is part of a large US-based conglomerate. Moon Digitizing started off as an in-house digitizing studio in the early ’90s, catering to the needs of various sister business units. It has since then evolved into a fully functional stand-alone entity that caters to the end of clients globally with sole focus on digitizing services; that is what we specialize in, and that is why our clients keep coming back to us. Our singular focus is on exceeding our clients’ expectations on all fronts. This philosophy has been ingrained in our people across all functions, from sales to customer service. We have a highly experienced team, with a cumulative experience in creating 25,000+ logos across a spectrum of types and difficulty/complexity levels. You can count on us to deliver every possible category of design: hat logos, left chest logos, jacket back designs, patches on various fabrics, textures, fashion apparel, and so on. Our team of digitizers works in close conjunction with our sales and customer service team to ensure a well-rounded and personalized experience for each client. What sets us apart is our ability to offer quality and service at prices and speeds that are unmatched in the marketplace.
