Simple Order Process

The order process consists of 4 basic steps: order placement, design approval, production, and order dispatch. Each step must be completed in order. We want you to be satisfied with your personalized patch. Your order information and personal information always remain confidential with us.

Order Placement

Place a new order by filling up the Order Form. The form should include your design in any graphics file format. Make sure you provide accurate information about the size of the patch. Make sure to clearly distinguish between the billing address and shipping address. Expect to receive a digital design proposal within 24 to 48 hours.

Design Approval

You have the privilege to make unlimited changes to the digital design proposal. Our designers will collaborate with you to ensure you are satisfied with the overall design. Your approval begins your personalized patch production. Contact us if you want to make changes to the design after approving the final design. Alterations to the design after a design approval are only made on a case to case basis.

Production

Patch production begins as soon as we receive your design approval. The standard turnaround time is about 14 business days. Orders of personalized patches with simple designs and a limited number of patches will be completed sooner. Creating personalized patches with complex designs of large sizes in the thousands can take longer.

Order Dispatch

Once patch production is complete, the job of the quality assurance department begins. All of the customized patches must meet the set quality control requirements. Customized patches that do not meet the quality criteria are replaced or redone. Receive your tracking number as soon as your order is complete.
