SPECIAL FEATURES OF OUR CUSTOM LEATHER PATCHES

Moon Patches produces customized leather patches with customizable designs. Our rates for premium leather patches are particularly competitive because all the leather processing takes place in-house. We can deboss, emboss, laser engrave, and laser-print custom designs on the leather patches!

Debossed Leather Patches

Debossed leather patches are created by applying heat and pressure on leather with a metal cast. Your customized design appears stamped on the leather patch.

Laser Engraved Leather Patches

Laser-engraved leather patches contain intricate designs because lasers engrave the design. The entire leather laser engraving process is automated. You can create custom-shaped leather patches with intricate designs through the laser engraving process!

Printed Leather Patches

Printed leather patch creation takes place with specially designed ink-jet printers for leather. Multicolored design creation takes place with ease on the leather patch. Printed leather patches are the perfect way to add an impressive flair to your wardrobe.

Embossed Leather Patches

Embossed leather patches are created by applying heat and pressure on damp leather with a metal cast. Your customized design appears stamped with raised outlines. Choose from blind embossing, color embossing, or gold embossing for a vintage high-quality custom leather patch.
