Unique Advantages Of Custom Dye Sublimated Patches

Custom dye-sublimated patches are the most ornate patches with photographic precision. Embroidered and woven patches can not achieve the same level of picture clarity as dye-sublimated patches. Dye sublimation printing is a heat transfer printing process done without sewing a single stitch. The sky's the limit to creating picture-perfect printed patches.

Color Gradients & Fine Text In Custom Dye Sublimated Patches

Dye Sublimated patches vividly associate multiple colors and color gradients with high resolutions at affordable rates. Dye sublimated patches can artistically include fine text and maintain clarity. Dye- sublimated patches can be washed and dry-cleaned without getting damaged.
