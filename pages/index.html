QUALITY PATCH TYPES TO CHOOSE FROM

We have a broad range of the best custom patches and materials, matching any style or preference.

EMBROIDERY

Classic never goes out of style

These patches are the ideal integration of traditional and modern style. Truly the perfect choice for using our variety of colors and colouring options in a creative way.

CHENILLE

Fuzzy and fashionable patches

Chenille patches have an iconic fuzzy texture and a lively appearance. The 3D effect combined with the energetic colour options lead to an all round chic patch.

PVC

Patches that were made to stand out

The three dimensional rubber appearance sets a flawless backdrop to the clean, sophisticated and animatedly colorful artwork on top– while staying a highly durable patch.

APPLIQUE

Classy combination patches

Made from multiple layers of high quality fabric, these trendy and finely detailed patches combine different colors, textures and materials in an effortless and stylish look.

WOVEN

A patch that redefines detail

Woven patches give an incredibly clean and crisp appearance by using a thinner thread onto fabric backing. They are Ideal for showcasing intricate detail with a high resolution effect.

SUBLIMATED

Photo realised patches

The best choice to get precise artwork from paper onto a patch. The finish of these patches are smooth, clean and meticulous- any image looks flawless on these trendy patches
